Lasting change starts on the inside and works outward. Every program moves people and organizations through five stages, so growth is not a one-time event but a system that holds.

How Enrollment Works

Join now, pay once there's a date

01

Join the waitlist

Pick a course and add your name. No payment is required to join, and you tell us which course you want.

02

A date is set

Once at least five people are interested, the cohort is scheduled and everyone on the waitlist is notified of the dates.

03

Pay securely with Square

Lock your seat with a secure Square payment once your course has a confirmed date. Your card details are never stored on this site.

Payments processed by Square. PCI-compliant and encrypted.

Payment and cancellation policy

Your payment reserves your seat. We do not offer refunds. If you cannot attend after registering, you may transfer your seat to someone else at no charge, or take a full credit toward any other Turner Training Institute™ course, valid for 12 months. If a course is rescheduled or cancelled by Turner Training Institute™, you may move to the next available session or take a full credit of equal value. Please let us know before the course begins.
